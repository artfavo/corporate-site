Marcel Duchamp Prize
Find all the information about the 2013 edition and many of the nominees and winners since 2000.
ArtFavo is launching with the help of ADIAF − the collector group that created the Marcel Duchamp prize in 2000. On this occasion, you will rediscover some of the prize history and the works of artists from galleries, collectors and institutions. A unique opportunity to get to know and discover the French art scene through some of its most recognized artists.
