THE MARCEL DUCHAMP PRIZE 2013 ON THE MOBILE APPLICATION ARTFAVO
On the occasion of the FIAC, ADIAF and its partner ARTFAVO propose to follow the key moments of the 2013 Marcel Duchamp Prize on a new mobile application created for enthusiasts and collectors of contemporary art.
The iPhone application ArtFavo proposes a visual tour of the four nominated artists: Farah ATASSI, Latifa ECHAKHCH, Claire FONTAINE and Raphael ZARKA, with images of their latest creations. The application will follow the debates and the award. A set of resources presents the Prize history since 2000 and artworks belonging to collectors and institutions. Available recent works of previous nominated artists and winners are also highlighted and provide an overview of current work from the French artistic scene.
Marcel Duchamp Award Ceremony
Nowadays with the expansion of social networks and 4G, ArtFavo stays on the cutting edge of technology to meet the Internet consumers needs. The quality of the images and the content make this application a real pleasure to use. The application is multilingual (English and French) and gives users access to:
  • Live information from Twitter encouraging openness to the world of art.
  • Videos and web links to broaden the vision of artistic proposals.
  • Management of favourites to follow artists and galleries.
  • Building virtual collections.
  • Available art works with availability, prices, visual and description.
  • Communication and sharing using social networks.
« ArtFavo helps all collectors and enthusiasts access and share information and images about contemporary art. Our association wants to develop the spirit of collector, thus contributing to the development of new players on the art scene. » said Gilles Fuchs, President of the ADIAF. « The application ArtFavo goes in the right direction. »
« It is an honour for ArtFavo to be the partner of ADIAF and the Marcel Duchamp Prize. We want to bring collectors and enthusiasts of contemporary art ever relevant, visually rich and permanently accessible art information on their mobile. Discovering new artists and their work is facilitated, the relationship with art professionals is done instantly. » said Pierre Baudin, President of ArtFavo.
About ADIAF and the MARCEL DUCHAMP prize - www.adiaf.com
The ADIAF Association for the International Diffusion of French Art groups 300 contemporary art collectors intensely engaged in the adventure of creation. Its mission is to contribute to the international influence of the French scene through the MARCEL DUCHAMP price which annually honours a French artist living in France and working in the field of visual arts, and educating the public about the vitality of contemporary art.
About ArtFavo
A French company founded in summer 2012 by art and Internet enthusiasts, our ambition is to create a reference mobile application for collectors and art lovers from around the world. They will find all the resources necessary to build an art collection of their liking, making real discoveries and new encounters with art professionals.
